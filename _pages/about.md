I am a freelance stratigraphic geologist. Independent consultant on environmental paleontology.

Research interests
======
I am interested in allostratigraphy in siliciclastic environments associated with the Andean margin, both marine and siliciclastic. I use applied ichnology to understand stratigraphic surfaces. I have conducted research on sedimentary dynamics in the Arauco Basin and the Central Depression Basin to understand allostratigraphic units and discontinuity surfaces. I have also collaborated on research related to the neotectonics of the Chilean forearc of the Nahuelbuta Range, as well as the marine neotectonics of the Magallanes-Fagnano Fault in southern Chile.
